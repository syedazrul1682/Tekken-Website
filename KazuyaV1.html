Kazuya Mishima

Devil Kazuya

COUNTRY: Japan
STYLE: Mishima Style Fighting Karate
BIRTHDAY: SEPTEMBER 10TH

Kazuya Mishima is a central antagonist and iconic character in Bandai Namco's Tekken series, known as a ruthless, power-hungry martial artist driven by revenge against his father, Heihachi. After being thrown off a cliff as a child, he embraced the demonic Devil Gene to survive, transforming into a formidable fighter who battles for control of the Mishima Zaibatsu and global domination.
